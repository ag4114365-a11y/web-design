ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad

1. Basic Information

  • Established in 2000 under Society for Educational Excellence
  • Located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh (Delhi-NCR Region)
  • Affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow
  • Approved by AICTE, New Delhi

2. Academic Programs

  1. Undergraduate Courses (B.Tech)
    • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
    • Information Technology (IT)
    • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
    • Mechanical Engineering (ME)
    • Civil Engineering (CE)
  2. Postgraduate Courses
    • MBA - Master of Business Administration
    • MCA - Master of Computer Applications
    • M.Tech in various specializations

3. Facilities

  • Modern classrooms with smart projectors
  • Central library with e-resources
  • Hostel and cafeteria for students
  • Sports ground and gym
  • Centers of Excellence (AI, Cyber Security, Data Science)
